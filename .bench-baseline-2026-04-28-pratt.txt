=== Pratt parser baseline (mindc 0.2.5, 2026-04-28) ===

Pratt operator-precedence parser replaces recursive-descent
chain (parse_logical_or → parse_logical_and → parse_comparison
→ parse_additive → parse_bitwise → parse_multiplicative).

Bench: cargo bench --bench compiler -- --quick

  small_matmul:   3.00 µs   (pre-Pratt 3.04, pre-10.5 2.887 → +3.9% vs original)
  medium_mlp:     6.13 µs   (pre-Pratt 6.74, pre-10.5 6.740 → -9.0% vs original)
  large_network: 16.82 µs   (pre-Pratt 18.29, pre-10.5 17.632 → -4.6% vs original)

Net: parser is faster than the original pre-Phase-10.5 baseline on
medium_mlp and large_network, and only +3.9% on small_matmul.

All 133 tests across 30 suites pass after the rewrite.
